Meet the Team

Shelly

Shelly loves to work with pups of all sizes. She is a recipient of the Ontario Grooming Achievement Award and her work has been featured in Modern Dog Magazine.

Penny

Penny has been a dog groomer at Riley's Pet Grooming for over 30 years. Penny is a certified dog trainer allowing her to accommodate dogs with separation anxiety and behavioral issues.
